Muktir Ghonta is a Youth organization, It is run by a few intellectual & idealistic youth personalities. Founded by Saad Al Amin & Naimur Rahman Talha on 14 November 2018. When we started Muktir Ghonta there were just 3 people. But after some time we're able to have 5 more members. And every single one of them is tremendously creative in different types of fields. Currently, we have more than 20 members, every single of them is insanely creative & productive. Those members are improving themselves day by day under the help & guidelines of Muktir Ghonta.
